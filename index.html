Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What marketplaces does Listo support? ▼

Listo supports Amazon, Flipkart, Shopify, eBay, and more. You can also paste any product URL or manually enter details for unsupported platforms.

How accurate is the SEO title generation? ▼

Our AI engine analyzes top‑performing listings and search trends to generate titles optimized for visibility. Titles typically improve click‑through rates by 20–35%.

Is my product data secure? ▼

Absolutely. We don't store any product data on our servers. All processing is done in real‑time and results are only available in your browser session.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼

Yes! There are no long‑term contracts. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from your account settings.

Do you offer an API? ▼

Yes, our Enterprise plan includes full API access with detailed documentation, allowing you to integrate Listo into your own tools and workflows.
